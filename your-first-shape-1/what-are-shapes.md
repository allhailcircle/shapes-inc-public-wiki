# What are Shapes?

Shapes can&#x20;

* Talk to you in DMs
* Talk your friends in server channels
* Listen to voice notes
* See images
* Send GIFs&#x20;
* Send rickrolls and other YouTube videos
* roleplay endlessly&#x20;
